A web-based platform designed to help you control and track social media habits.

You can track anything. From time spent on the app to number of posts.

Go to the Tracker page and choose "Set New Track". Fill in the requested information. You can also set personal goals for this tracked behavior and see your progress on the page.

The timer is a tool you can use to limit your time spent on social media. Once a timer has been set, it will countdown when the selected site has been opened. As the timer approached zero, a notification will pop up indicating your time is almost up. When the timer runs out, you can set it so that it will close the site automatically.

You can set important deadlines such as tests, projects, work deadlines, etc. and we'll remind you when your deadlines is approaching! With this tool, you're able to see if you're spending too much time on social media when you have work to get done.

The details of your social media habits can be found by clicking on the desired website on your homepage.
